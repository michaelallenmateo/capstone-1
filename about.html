DrunknPub

Established in 2018, DrunknPub is located at the rooftop of Guesswhere Building designed to accomodate a capacity of 500pax.

DrunknPub is the best place to go on a night just to relax, dance, enjoy variety of drinks and music. From 6pm to 5am on weekdays, expect nothing but the best entertainment experience with our live band performances and the best music mixes by our best DJs during weekends.

So what are you waiting for? Come and enjoy the night with us!
